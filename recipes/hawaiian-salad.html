Hawaiian-Inspired Watergate Salad

Description

Similar to a Watergate salad, with some of the ingredients changed.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®), thawed
  • 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple in juice
  • 1/4 cup instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 teaspoons instant banana pudding mix, or to taste
  • 3 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 1 banana, sliced, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Mix whipped topping, crushed pineapple with juice, vanilla pudding mix, and banana pudding mix together in a mixing bowl. Add more banana pudding mix until desired flavor is reached. Stir in marshmallows. Serve with sliced banana on top.
